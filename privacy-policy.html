Your privacy is our priority. BloxyFlix is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to our servers.

1. Introduction

BloxyFlix ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Bloxy Play Technology & Blocky Studio Company Limited. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to protecting your privacy and how we handle information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

BloxyFlix operates on a privacy-first architecture. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information on our servers. The only data associated with your use is stored locally on your own device using your browser's localStorage feature.

Locally Stored Data

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Watch History — A record of videos you have watched, stored to enable the "Continue Watching" feature
  • Watchlist — Videos you have saved for later viewing
  • Playback Preferences — Your preferred video quality, playback speed, and volume settings
  • Display Settings — Theme preferences, language settings, and accessibility options
  • Display Name — An optional name you set for personalization (used only for comments)
  • Ratings & Comments — Your interactions with content, stored locally

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • IP addresses or geographic location data
  • Device identifiers or browser fingerprints
  • Browsing history or behavior analytics
  • Cookies or tracking pixels
  • Any data that could personally identify you

4. How Your Data is Stored

All your data stays on your device. We use your browser's localStorage API to store preferences and history. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We have no access to your viewing history or preferences
  • You have complete control over your data

5. Third-Party Services

BloxyFlix embeds content from third-party platforms. Please be aware that:

  • YouTube — Videos hosted on YouTube are subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • CDN Services — Content delivery networks may collect technical data for performance optimization
  • Streaming Sources — Live streams from external sources operate under their own privacy policies

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services separately.

6. Children's Privacy

BloxyFlix is an educational archive intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Data Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access — Your data is always accessible to you through the app interface
  • Deletion — Clear your data anytime via Settings → Data & Privacy
  • Portability — Export your watchlist and history through browser developer tools
  • Control — Disable data collection entirely by using private/incognito browsing

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your BloxyFlix data at any time:

  • Go to Settings → Data & Privacy to clear specific data types
  • Clear your browser's localStorage/site data to remove all BloxyFlix data
  • Use your browser's private/incognito mode to prevent data storage entirely

8. Security

While we do not store your data on our servers, we implement security measures to protect the integrity of our platform:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits of our codebase
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) to prevent XSS attacks

9. International Users

BloxyFlix is accessible worldwide. By using our service, you acknowledge that your locally stored data is subject to the laws of your jurisdiction, not ours.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify users through the platform where practicable
